*javax.security.auth.login.AccountExpiredException_Description*

Signals that a user account has expired. 

This exception is thrown by LoginModules when they determine that an account 
has expired. For example, a LoginModule, after successfully authenticating a 
user, may determine that the user's account has expired. In this case the 
LoginModule throws this exception to notify the application. The application 
can then take the appropriate steps to notify the user.
