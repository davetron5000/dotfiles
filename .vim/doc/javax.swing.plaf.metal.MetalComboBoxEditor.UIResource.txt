*javax.swing.plaf.metal.MetalComboBoxEditor.UIResource_Description*

A subclass of BasicComboBoxEditor that implements UIResource. 
BasicComboBoxEditor doesn't implement UIResource directly so that applications 
can safely override the cellRenderer property with BasicListCellRenderer 
subclasses.
