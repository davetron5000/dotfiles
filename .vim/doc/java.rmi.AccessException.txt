*java.rmi.AccessException_Description*

An AccessException is thrown by certain methods of the java.rmi.Naming class 
(specifically bind, rebind, and unbind) and methods of the 
java.rmi.activation.ActivationSystem interface to indicate that the caller does 
not have permission to perform the action requested by the method call. If the 
method was invoked from a non-local host, then an AccessException is thrown.
