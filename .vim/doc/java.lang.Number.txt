*java.lang.Number_Description*

The abstract class Number is the superclass of classes BigDecimal, BigInteger, 
Byte, Double, Float, Integer, Long, and Short. 

Subclasses of Number must provide methods to convert the represented numeric 
value to byte, double, float, int, long, and short.
