An editor for a JSpinner whose model is a SpinnerListModel. 


*javax.swing.JSpinner.ListEditor(JSpinner)*

public JSpinner.ListEditor(javax.swing.JSpinner spinner)

Construct a JSpinner editor that supports displaying and editing the value of a 
SpinnerListModel with a JFormattedTextField. This ListEditor becomes both a 
ChangeListener on the spinner and a PropertyChangeListener on the new 
JFormattedTextField. 

    spinner - the spinner whose model this editor will monitor
