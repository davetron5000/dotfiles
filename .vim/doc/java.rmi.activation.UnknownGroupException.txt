*java.rmi.activation.UnknownGroupException_Description*

An UnknownGroupException is thrown by methods of classes and interfaces in the 
java.rmi.activation package when the ActivationGroupID parameter to the method 
is determined to be invalid, i.e., not known by the ActivationSystem. An 
UnknownGroupException is also thrown if the ActivationGroupID in an 
ActivationDesc refers to a group that is not registered with the 
ActivationSystem
