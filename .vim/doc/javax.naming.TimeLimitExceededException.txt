*javax.naming.TimeLimitExceededException_Description*

This exception is thrown when a method does not terminate within the specified 
time limit. This can happen, for example, if the user specifies that the method 
should take no longer than 10 seconds, and the method fails to complete with 10 
seconds. 

Synchronization and serialization issues that apply to NamingException apply 
directly here.
