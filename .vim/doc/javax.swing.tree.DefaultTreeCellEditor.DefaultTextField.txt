*javax.swing.tree.DefaultTreeCellEditor.DefaultTextField_Description*

TextField used when no editor is supplied. This textfield locks into the border 
it is constructed with. It also prefers its parents font over its font. And if 
the renderer is not null and no font has been specified the preferred height is 
that of the renderer.
