*javax.swing.plaf.basic.BasicPasswordFieldUI_Description*

Provides the Windows look and feel for a password field. The only difference 
from the standard text field is that the view of the text is simply a string of 
the echo character as specified in JPasswordField, rather than the real text 
contained in the field.
