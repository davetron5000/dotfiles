public void flavorsChanged(java.awt.datatransfer.FlavorEvent e)

Invoked when the target (|java.awt.datatransfer.Clipboard|) of the listener has 
changed its available (|java.awt.datatransfer.DataFlavor|) s. 

Some notifications may be redundant they are not caused by a change of the set 
of DataFlavors available on the clipboard. For example, if the clipboard 
subsystem supposes that the system clipboard's contents has been changed but it 
can't ascertain whether its DataFlavors have been changed because of some 
exceptional condition when accessing the clipboard, the notification is sent to 
ensure from omitting a significant notification. Ordinarily, those redundant 
notifications should be occasional.
