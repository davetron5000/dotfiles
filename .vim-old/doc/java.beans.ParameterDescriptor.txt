*java.beans.ParameterDescriptor_Description*

The ParameterDescriptor class allows bean implementors to provide additional information on each of their parameters, beyond the low level type information provided by the 
java.lang.reflect.Method class. 

Currently all our state comes from the FeatureDescriptor base class.
