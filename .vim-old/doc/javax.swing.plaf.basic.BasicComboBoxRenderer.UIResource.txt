*javax.swing.plaf.basic.BasicComboBoxRenderer.UIResource_Description*

A subclass of BasicComboBoxRenderer that implements UIResource. BasicComboBoxRenderer doesn't implement UIResource directly so that applications can safely override the 
cellRenderer property with BasicListCellRenderer subclasses.
