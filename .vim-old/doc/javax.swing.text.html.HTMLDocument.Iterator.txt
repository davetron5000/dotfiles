*javax.swing.text.html.HTMLDocument.Iterator_Description*

An iterator to iterate over a particular type of tag. The iterator is not thread safe. If reliable access to the document is not already ensured by the context under which the 
iterator is being used, its use should be performed under the protection of Document.render.
