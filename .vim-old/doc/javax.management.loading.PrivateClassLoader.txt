*javax.management.loading.PrivateClassLoader_Description*

Marker interface indicating that a ClassLoader should not be added to the 
(|javax.management.loading.ClassLoaderRepository|) . When a ClassLoader is 
registered as an MBean in the MBean server, it is added to the MBean server's 
ClassLoaderRepository unless it implements this interface.
