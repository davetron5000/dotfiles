*javax.swing.SwingConstants_Description*

A collection of constants generally used for positioning and orienting components on the screen. 



*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.BOTTOM*

Box-orientation constant used to specify the bottom of a box. 


*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.CENTER*

The central position in an area. Used for both compass-direction constants (NORTH, etc.) and box-orientation constants (TOP, etc.). 


*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.EAST*

Compass-direction east (right). 


*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.HORIZONTAL*

Horizontal orientation. Used for scrollbars and sliders. 


*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.LEADING*

Identifies the leading edge of text for use with left-to-right and right-to-left languages. Used by buttons and labels. 


*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.LEFT*

Box-orientation constant used to specify the left side of a box. 


*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.NEXT*

Identifies the next direction in a sequence. 


*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.NORTH*

Compass-direction North (up). 


*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.NORTH_EAST*

Compass-direction north-east (upper right). 


*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.NORTH_WEST*

Compass-direction north west (upper left). 


*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.PREVIOUS*

Identifies the previous direction in a sequence. 


*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.RIGHT*

Box-orientation constant used to specify the right side of a box. 


*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.SOUTH*

Compass-direction south (down). 


*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.SOUTH_EAST*

Compass-direction south-east (lower right). 


*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.SOUTH_WEST*

Compass-direction south-west (lower left). 


*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.TOP*

Box-orientation constant used to specify the top of a box. 


*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.TRAILING*

Identifies the trailing edge of text for use with left-to-right and right-to-left languages. Used by buttons and labels. 


*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.VERTICAL*

Vertical orientation. Used for scrollbars and sliders. 


*int_javax.swing.SwingConstants.WEST*

Compass-direction west (left).
